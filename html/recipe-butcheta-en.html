Baked chicken drumsticks with white rice
and parsley in the oven

For 6 portions:

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 onion
  • 2 sprigs of parsley
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • 2 tbs oil

Method of preparation:

The chicken drumsticks (6 pieces) are washed and boiled until ready in salted water. The broth from their boiling is saved.

In a pan, stew the finely chopped onion (1) until soft. As soon as it softens, add the washed rice (1 teaspoon). It is fried until the rice becomes transparent. Then season with salt and pepper. Stir and after 3-4 minutes remove from heat.

The rice is distributed evenly in a pan, and the cooked chicken legs are arranged on it. The dish is poured with the broth from the chicken, and it is necessary to observe the 3:1 water-rice ratio. The dish is baked at a temperature of 180 degrees until the rice absorbs the liquid.

The chicken drumsticks with rice are served hot and sprinkled with fresh parsley.

Preparation time: 60 minutes
