Breaded mozzarella cheese balls

For 4 portions:

  • 300 g of baby mozzarella
  • 50 g of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 50 g breadcrumbs
  • 300 ml oil for frying
  • salt

Method of preparation:

The mozzarella balls are drained from the water and dried. Roll in flour and sprinkle with salt. Then in egg and finally in breadcrumbs.

They are dipped in portions in hot oil and fried for a few minutes until a crispy crust is obtained.

They are best served with blueberry jam.

Preparation time: 20 minutes
