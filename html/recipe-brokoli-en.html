Breaded Broccoli

For 4 portions:

  • 1 head broccoli
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 g of corn flour
  • 100 g of white flour
  • salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 coffee cup oil

Method of preparation:

Broccoli is divided into florets and boiled for 3-4 minutes in boiling water. Immediately after, they are cooled in ice water. This shock cooling will preserve their fresh green color.

Beat the eggs in a bowl along with some salt and pepper. Mix the corn and white flour separately in a bowl.

Heat the oil in a pan, roll the broccoli florets first in the dry mixture, then in the one with the eggs and then again in the dry mixture. Then they are dropped into the hot fat and breaded evenly on both sides.

Breaded broccoli is served warm with garlic yogurt.

Preparation time: 35 minutes
