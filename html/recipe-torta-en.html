Easy Tiramisu Cake with Plain Biscuits
and Creme Sabayon for Dessert

For 1 portion:

  • 75 g biscuits
  • 37 1/2 ml coffee
  • 1/2 tbs sugar
  • 25 ml marsala wine

    • for creme sabayon:

  • 20 g egg yolks
  • 25 g sugar
  • 30 ml marsala wine
  • 100 g mascarpone cheese
  • 43 3/4 ml cream
  • 1/4 tbs cocoa

Method of preparation:

The sugar is dissolved in the warm coffee and allowed to cool. Add the Marsala wine and stir.

The egg yolks and sugar are beaten with a mixer and the bowl is placed in a water bath. Add the Marsala wine and stir the cream until it thickens. The finished cream is removed from the heat and placed in a larger bowl filled with ice. Stir until cool.

Beat the mascarpone with the cooled cream and finally add the whipped cream.

Arrange biscuits dipped in coffee in a suitable form. When a row is arranged, they are smeared with cream. Then more biscuits and cream again. The dessert is ordered in this way until the quantities are exhausted.

It ends with a layer of cream that is smoothed and sprinkled with cocoa. The tiramisu is covered with transparent foil and left in the refrigerator overnight.

Preparation time: 50 minutes
