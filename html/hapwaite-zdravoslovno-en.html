Diet "Health". Eat healthy foods

Balanced nutrition

The reasons for gaining weight can be very different. Very often it is not only due to excessive nutrition, but also to depressions, frequent illnesses, use of many drugs. These conditions can often be corrected with a proper diet. This is exactly the purpose of the healthy diet that we have prepared for you. It is suitable for people with an extremely hectic and stressful daily life, as it helps in the fight against extra pounds. This diet and the correct nutritional regime generally protect against more serious diseases.


Which foods should we avoid and which should we turn to?

Suitable for this diet:

  • fatty sea fish - salmon, tuna, mackerel, herring (one of the main ingredients of this diet)
  • peas, beans, walnuts (indispensable sources of vegetable proteins)
  • vegetables (best with green leaves) and fruits (with a small amount of sugar) in large quantities
  • extra virgin cold-pressed olive oil (not to be used for frying)
  • low-fat dairy products
  • white meat such as skinless turkey breast (this is a way to avoid saturated fatty acids)

To avoid:

  • corn, sunflower and soybean oil
  • products fried with fat
  • margarine
  • factory-made sweets (biscuits, chips, waffles, snacks) are strictly prohibited.


Diet against depression

This diet is for all overtired and weighed down by depression and melancholy people. It relies on fresh fruits and vegetables, as well as unsaturated fatty acids. This diet is very balanced and supplies large amounts of antioxidants, vitamins and minerals, cellulose and salicylates and makes the body more resistant to disease. Fats, such as olive oil and canola oil, which are rich in omega 3 acids and antioxidants are a very important part of the diet. Carbohydrates from whole grains and type foods are also allowed and complement the diet well. It is not allowed to sweeten drinks.
