TASTY HOMEMADE PANCAKES - CLASSIC RECIPE

For 10 portions:

  • 1/2 L milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 250 g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 pinch of salt

Method of preparation:

From the milk, eggs, flour, baking powder, melted butter and salt, a pancake batter with a consistency slightly thinner than bosa is whipped. Pancakes are baked from the dough until the quantity is used up. About 10-12 pancakes should be obtained from the indicated products.

Always be careful when making pancakes, a few seconds of inattention and they can burn.

Preparation time: 40 minutes
