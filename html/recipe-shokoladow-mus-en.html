Dark chocolate mousse with mascarpone
and coffee

For 4 portions:

  • 100 g dark chocolate
  • 25 g mascarpone
  • 150 ml pastry cream
  • 2 tbs powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp instant coffee

Method of preparation:

Dark chocolate is crushed and melted in a water bath. Then let it cool slightly.

Beat the mascarpone and powdered sugar. Add the instant coffee and then the melted chocolate.

Whip the pastry cream until it thickens. It is added in parts to the other mixture. The cream is stirred and distributed in bowls or glasses.

The dessert is left in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. The mousse is then garnished as desired and served.

Preparation time: 20 minutes
