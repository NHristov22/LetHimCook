Crispy spicy oven-baked country
style potatoes

For 4 portions:

  • 1/2 kg potatoes
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • salt
  • 1 tsp savory
  • garlic powder
  • spicy paprika
  • 3 tbs oil

Method of preparation:

Wash the potatoes very well with warm water and cut lengthwise into thick pieces without peeling them.

In the pan greased with oil, which will be used for baking, mix all the spices and roll the potatoes in the mixture.

Bake in a well-heated oven at 200 degrees for 30 minutes, until they turn red and a crispy shell is obtained.

They are served as an appetizer or main course, combined with a salad, prepared meat or sauce.

Preparation time: 50 minutes
